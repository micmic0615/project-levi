0.2.6b Nov 12 2018
  • removed right click shield function
  • right click now stops time
    • can only be used when charge is full
    • costs life per second
    • attacking in time-stop state also costs life
    • rally is frozen during time stop
    • dash is unuseable during time stop
0.2.6 Nov 11 2018
  • changed dash control scheme
    • from right click to spacebar/double tap wasd
    • pressing spacebar will trigger dash in your current facing direction
    • double tapping wasd will trigger dash in one of the four cardinal directions
  • limited dashing to three consecutive dashes (it will go in a 1 second cooldown afterwards)
  • nerfed dash bullets
    • significanty nerfed dash bullet damage
    • dash bullets now come in 3/4/5 waves instead of constantly shooting 5 bullets
    • dash bullets now have zero poise damage
  • significantly buffed movespeed (200 -> 600)
    • on-dash/on-attack speedboost nerfed (800 -> 600)
    • total boosted movespeed is now 1200 (from 1000)
    • on-dash/on-attack speedboost is now only given by the 1st attack (of your 4 attack combo) and the 1st dash (of your 3 dash combo)
  • holding the attack button (left click) will now continuosly volley attacks instead of charging your attack
  • rebalanced left click attack health and poise damages
    • 1st shot now deals significantly more poise damage but retains its normal health damage
    • 2nd, 3rd & 4th volleys now deal significantly more health damage but also deals insignificant poise damage
    • reduced the spread angle for the 2nd, 3rd and 4th volleys
  • charging is now done automatically when the player isn't doing anything but moving
    • charging needs 4 seconds of not doing anything (except wasd movement) to reach full charge
    • using any of the action functions (dash, attack, shield) will consume the charge and reset it back to zero
    • charge scales left click health and poise damages as well as bullet speed and hitbox
    • maximum charge left click will emit a large green explosion that deals poise damage in a big area (potentially stunning everything in that area)
    • maximum charge dash will give a 1.5 second invulnerability shield
  • added a placeholder shield function in the right click
    • gives a 1.5 second (2 seconds if fully charged) invulnerability shield
    • triggers the on-kill machine gun function
    • requires at least 50% of your poise to use
    • this consumes the entirety of your poise (if you get hit after the shield expires, you will most likely be stunned)
  • attacking stunned enemies now deals 25% more damage
  • nerfed the treshold and cooldown of the on-hit invulnerability shield function, but slightly buffed its duration
  • added a rally mechanic to synergize with the on-hit and grit shield functions
    • 6% limited lifesteal
  • Minor rebalances to the enemy type, The Swarm
